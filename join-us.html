About the Show

Virtually Speaking has been the trusted voice of the virtualization and cloud infrastructure community for over a decade. Each week, our hosts bring in the brightest minds — subject matter experts from Broadcom, VMware, and across the broader industry — for candid, technically rich conversations that practitioners and decision-makers actually want to hear.

We cover everything at the intersection of VMware Cloud Foundation, vSphere, vSAN, NSX, AI infrastructure, and the modern enterprise IT ecosystem. Our audience is made up of hands-on IT professionals, cloud architects, storage engineers, and technology leaders who rely on this show to stay sharp and stay ahead.

What It's Like to Be a Guest

Being on Virtually Speaking is low-pressure and genuinely fun. No gotcha questions, no scripts, no awkward surprises. Here's what to expect:

1

Pre-Show Alignment

We connect ahead of time to agree on topics so you show up confident and prepared.

2

Conversational Style

We talk with you, not at you. Think two colleagues having a relaxed, smart discussion.

  • Reach a Targeted, Technically Sophisticated Audience Our viewers are practitioners — VMware admins, cloud architects, and storage engineers actively making technology decisions. This is not a general tech show; it's built for the people your message matters to most.

  • A Decade of Credibility — and a #1 Award to Match Over 10 years of consistent, quality content. Voted the #1 technical podcast in the virtualization community through an industry-wide vote. That trust and longevity transfers to every guest who appears on the show.
